About Me

The year of studying in Hong Kong has been very special to me, and being able to document it in the form of a webpage is a fresh attempt. This chapter of my graduate life is coming to an end. I have regrets about missing out on the second-class scholarship by only 0.7 points, but I also rejoice in getting to know many talented classmates and finding satisfaction in furthering my undergraduate major. After completing my undergraduate studies, I felt that I lacked the skills needed for the computer industry. Fortunately, I pursued AIDM, hoping to find a path within the "computer industry" that seems suitable for me.
